Shipping from China to the UAE and GCC

China has firmly established itself as the leading global supplier of products and goods, with no real competition when it comes to serving the markets of the United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, the Gulf countries, and the broader Middle East. If you're in search of a reliable and efficient air or sea cargo partner from China, Al-Fares Cargo Services is your trusted choice for fast, secure, and professional shipping solutions from China to destinations around the world.

At Al-Fares, we take pride in our experienced and dedicated team who are committed to delivering excellence in logistics. Leveraging our deep expertise in both sea and air freight operations, we provide comprehensive shipping services from major cities in China directly to key locations such as Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Sharjah in the UAE, as well as to Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, and the Sultanate of Oman.

Whether you’re a business importing goods or an individual with specialized shipping needs, we ensure a smooth, timely, and cost-effective logistics experience tailored to your requirements.

Air shipping from China to UAE, Gulf countries and Middle East:

Air freight from China has become one of the most vital, fastest, and most flexible logistics services today, thanks to a wide range of available options and relatively competitive prices. As a leading air cargo provider from China to Dubai and beyond, Al Fares Cargo Services offers customized, cost-effective solutions from all major international airports across China.

In our commitment to support business growth and sustainability, we’ve enhanced our digital services and optimized our air freight rates to help reduce shipping and import costs as much as possible.

With years of experience in handling daily air cargo operations and commercial logistics, and a strong presence in key Chinese cities, we ensure a smooth, efficient, and affordable flow of goods. Our strong ties with major international airlines in China allow us to deliver unmatched speed and reliability.

Air freight is the ideal choice for shipments under 400 kg or less than 3 cubic meters, offering the fastest and safest way to transport goods.
